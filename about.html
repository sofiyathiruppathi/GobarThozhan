About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

We are a group of passionate college students working on a project to bring simple yet effective biogas solutions to rural communities. Our mission is to improve the digestion process and maximize the yield of biogas using smart control systems and instrumentation. By incorporating advanced sensors and other equipment, we aim to provide a more efficient and user-friendly way to manage small biogas plants.

Through GobarThozhan, we offer an easy-to-use control system that automates gas flow, monitors gas content, and helps optimize biogas production. Our goal is to support villages in becoming self-sufficient by harnessing renewable energy from biogas, while improving living standards and promoting sustainability.

We believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems, and our website serves as a platform to share knowledge, tools, and resources to help communities adopt biogas solutions easily.

Join us in our journey to make renewable energy accessible and affordable for everyone!.
